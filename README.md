## About Me

I am currently completing my **MSc in Molecular Biology and Genomics** at **Redeemer’s University, Ede, Nigeria**, with a background in **Microbiology** from **Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife, Nigeria**.

My interests sit at the intersection of:

- Microbial Genomics  
- Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR)  
- Host–Microbe Interactions  
- Bioinformatics & Computational Biology  
- Cancer Biology and Epigenetics  

Over time, I have become increasingly interested in how molecular and computational approaches can help us better understand infectious diseases, microbial evolution, and biological systems at scale.

Outside research, I enjoy scientific discussions, mentorship, teaching, and building communities that make science more accessible to students and early-career researchers.
